These dashboards provide an overview on the status of a set of metrics which District {{ snap.district_id }} Administrators will use in making the determination of whether it is necessary to move to a more restrictive position with respect to COVID-19 response and management.

While the decision to open/close schools rests with the administration, these dashboards should not be considered an indicator of the status of schools being open/closed.

The ultimate decision to open/close schools will be made:
  • By reviewing these metrics with respect to prevailing conditions
  • With guidance from prevailing authorities:
    • Centers for Disease Control
    • Illinois State Board of Education
    • ILlinois Department of Public Health
    • Lake County Department of Public Health
  • In consulatation with:
    • School Administrators
    • Local District Administrative Counterparts
    • Educator Union Representatives
    • Parent Representatives
    • Individual Experts and Professionals
